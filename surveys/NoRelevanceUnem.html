Invitation to Participate: You are invited to participate in this research study about fairness in machine learning using Amazon Mechanical Turk.

Purpose of the Study: From this research we wish to learn how human fairness perceptions in machine learning and decision making differ across contexts. Your task is to read each situation and label the use of certain information as fair or unfair.
